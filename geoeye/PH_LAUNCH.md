# Product Hunt 发布文案

## Tagline
See your website through the eyes of ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews

## Description
geoeye is an open-source CLI tool that analyzes any URL and tells you how likely AI search engines are to cite your content.

Unlike traditional SEO tools that optimize for Google's crawler, geoeye measures what AI models actually care about: structure clarity, content depth, freshness signals, and citability.

Run it on any page, get a score out of 100, and see exactly what to fix. No API keys. No accounts. No fees.

Built for the shift from search engines to answer engines.

## First comment
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

AI search is eating traditional search fast. ChatGPT now answers more queries than some search engines. Perplexity is growing 40% month-over-month. Google is pushing AI Overviews above organic results.

But nobody knows how to optimize for AI search.

I built geoeye because I was frustrated. There are 1000 SEO tools but zero GEO tools. So I wrote one.

It's simple: give it a URL, it gives you a score (0-100) and tells you what to fix. Six dimensions: title quality, meta description, structure hierarchy, content depth, freshness signals, and citation network.

It's MIT licensed, written in pure Python (no dependencies beyond stdlib), and takes 2 seconds to run.

Would love feedback — especially from content marketers and founders who are starting to see AI traffic in their analytics. What else should geoeye measure?
